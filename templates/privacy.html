Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps, used solely for security and debugging.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve the Service:

  • To display your expenses and spending summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls to protect your information.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Deletions are processed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) that process data on our behalf under confidentiality agreements.

Any third-party services we use are contractually required to handle your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws and solely for the purpose of delivering the Service.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses session cookies strictly to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser, but doing so will prevent you from staying signed in.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
